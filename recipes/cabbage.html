Stuffed Cabbages | محشي كرنب

Description

While not as amazing as the grape leaves variant, it's still amazing..

Completely vegan, healthy(in moderation), and absolutely delicious Egyptian staple.

Ingredients

  • Green Cabbages
  • A large white onion
  • Vegetable stock
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh herbs. Parsley, mint, and coriander
  • Tomato sauce and paste
  • Salt and pepper, and if you have them بهارات

Steps

  1. Prep the cabbage leaves
  2. Blanche the leaves(and try to ignore the smell)
  3. Mix the rice with onions, tomato, and spices
  4. Stuff the cabbage leaves
  5. Cook in vegetable stock
  6. Enjoy
